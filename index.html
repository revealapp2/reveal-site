Our Story

Our journey began after encountering repeated mysterious defeats, exposing a hidden flaw in poker apps. With determination, we uncovered a critical vulnerability in popular poker platforms like PPPOKER and XPoker. In November 2023, we launched the Reveal App for Poker, giving players unprecedented insights by allowing them to reveal their opponents' cards. Since its launch, our app has empowered players globally, providing a strategic edge and leveling the playing field. Now, you can take control of your game like never before!
